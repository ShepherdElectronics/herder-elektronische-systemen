04Featured public work

Controls at
testbed scale.

Public university research and engineering work completed within the Michigan Tech electric propulsion laboratory.

TI Piccolo dual-motor PMSM dynamometer

This public testbed brings together two PMSMs, TI C2000 F28069M control hardware, field-oriented control, QEP feedback, SCI-to-CAN communications and Speedgoat/MATLAB real-time testing.

Jonathan’s contribution

Jonathan contributed across embedded control, motor-drive integration, CAN communications, real-time testing, debugging and validation.

Evidence and methods

The repository includes architecture documentation, hardware integration notes, MATLAB/Python processing, efficiency maps and measured speed, torque and power data.

Publication boundary

This work was completed in a university and laboratory setting. It is not a private client engagement or an exclusively owned product. The site links to the public repository without reproducing its models or large source files.
