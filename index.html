testbed scale.

TI Piccolo dual-motor PMSM dynamometer

A public testbed integrating two permanent-magnet synchronous motors, TI C2000 F28069M control hardware, field-oriented control, QEP encoder feedback, SCI-to-CAN migration and Speedgoat/MATLAB real-time test integration.

Jonathan’s contribution

Contributions spanning embedded controls, motor-drive integration, CAN communications, real-time testing, debugging and validation.

Evidence and methods

The repository documents architecture, hardware integration, MATLAB/Python processing, efficiency mapping and measured speed, torque and power behavior.

Publication boundary

This is university and laboratory work, not a private client engagement or exclusively owned product. The website links to the public repository rather than duplicating models or large source artifacts.
