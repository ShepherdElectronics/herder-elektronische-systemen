Service 02 / Power conversion

Power electronics
proven on the bench.

We support low-power converter prototypes from architecture through bench bring-up, using measured behavior to guide the next design decision.

This work is useful when switching behavior, component selection, transformer design or incomplete measurements are slowing the next revision.

Start a technical discussion →
01Capabilities

Turn waveforms
into decisions.

Design and bring-up

  • Converter architecture and low-power SMPS development
  • Flyback topology and magnetics/transformer design support
  • Component selection, snubbers and switching behavior
  • PCB design review and prototype bring-up
  • EMI considerations within the prototype scope

Measurement and validation

  • Oscilloscope measurements and differential probing
  • Electronic-load testing and LCR measurements
  • Waveform review, bench notes and validation reporting
  • High-voltage safety and validation where applicable
  • Clear boundaries around energized hardware and test conditions

03Typical deliverables

Evidence for the
next revision.
