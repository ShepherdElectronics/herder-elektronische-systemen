This page is a non-legal placeholder for engagement principles and should be reviewed with appropriate legal advice before public launch.

Scope and deliverables

Engineering work is defined by an agreed scope, assumptions, deliverables, responsibilities and acceptance criteria. A project discussion or portfolio example does not create an engagement by itself.

Technical decisions

Recommendations depend on the available requirements, hardware, software, measurements and constraints. Any performance or validation statement should be tied to documented test conditions and results.

Representative work

Public projects are representative technical work and are not necessarily client engagements. They should not be interpreted as guarantees of a particular result, certification, production deployment or manufacturing capacity.

Confidentiality

Project details are handled confidentially. Public portfolio material is published only when authorized.
