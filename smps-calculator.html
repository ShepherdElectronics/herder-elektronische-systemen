What these tools do—and do not—establish

These calculators apply simplified, topology-specific first-pass equations. They do not establish safety, insulation coordination, component qualification, switch-node behavior, leakage effects, snubber design, controller limits, loop stability, thermal performance, EMI, layout suitability, manufacturing repeatability or regulatory compliance. A converter should be reviewed and tested against its actual controller, components, magnetics and operating conditions before it is built or released.
